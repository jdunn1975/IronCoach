Women's Health & Nutrition50s Focus
🌡️ Race Day Heat
Chattanooga in May averages 75–85°F. Estrogen changes reduce heat tolerance — start pre-cooling 2 weeks out. Cold towel on neck before the bike, ice in your kit on the run. Train midday once a week to acclimatize.
🦴 Bone & Joint Load
6 marathons = high cumulative bone stress. Estrogen decline accelerates bone loss — your 2×/week strength work is essential. Prioritize hip hinge and single-leg exercises. Watch for shin or foot pain as bike volume builds.
😴 Sleep & Recovery
At 10h/week training load, target 8–9h sleep. Night sweats can fragment sleep — keep bedroom cool (65–68°F). If HRV drops 15%+ from baseline, take a rest day. Sleep is where adaptation happens.
💊 Key Supplements
Vitamin D3 (2000 IU with fat), Magnesium glycinate (300mg before bed), Omega-3 (1–2g/day), Iron (check levels — endurance women often deficient). Always discuss with your physician before starting.
